Imperial College London Memory Research Group

At the Imperial College Memory Research Centre led by Dr Paul Edison, we focus our research on Alzheimer's disease, mild cognitive impairment, and causes of dementia and cognitive impairment.

We evaluate neuroinflammation and neurodegeneration using PET, MRI, and CSF markers. We are now evaluating the relationship between Neuroinflammation, Tau, glucose metabolism, and structural and functional changes measured by MRI. We heavily focus on basic and translational imaging using PET and MRI. We are evaluating novel therapeutic targets in dementia and cognitive impairment in Phase 1 and Phase 2/3 studies.
